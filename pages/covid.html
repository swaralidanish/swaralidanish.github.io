COVID Safety Protocols

ANTHEM PUNE strives to make each guest feel comfortable and protected during this difficult time. As a result, we've devised a 9-step cleaning and safety routine to address the health concerns that you may have when travelling. To maintain the greatest level of cleanliness in guest rooms and public areas, our hotels use disinfection and cleaning techniques.

1. Physical distancing

Implement physical distancing measures throughout the hotel.

2. Increase cleaning

Increase cleaning and disinfecting frequency throughout the hotel.

3. Air circulation

Improve air circulation processes to increase air quality.

4. Protective barrier

Install protective screens at the front desk and wherever necessary.

5. Sanitizing stations

Install alcohol-based hand sanitizing near the front entrance and public areas.

6. Disinfect key cards

Provide clean and disinfected key cards upon check-in.

7. Door hangers

Display door hanger with cleaning and disinfecting information.

8. Hand sanitizer

Provide each guest room with a travel-size hand sanitizer.

9. Food safety

Adhere to the strict safety procedures while serving all food and beverages.
